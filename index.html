The Book

An Invitation to Explore Our Boundless Mindscapes

As we move from a simple world dominated by material realities to an increasingly complex and virtual one, many old explanations, traditions, and practices are becoming outdated. So, which are still valid, and which are best left behind?

The Laws of ah! takes you on an informal yet novel tour of reality and the spectrum of human endeavors—with one eye on matter and the other on meaning, including the spiritual realms, the ongoing mystical revival, and our newly emerging cosmology.

By redefining the roles of science, politics, religion, and spirituality, it takes a fresh look at the major institutions that claim to rule our lives.

About the Author

Michael I. Christie

Organismal Biology Behavioral Ecology Transpersonal Psychology Social Psychology

Born in New York, Michael grew up in a bicultural family in Argentina — navigating the tension between a rising scientific-materialist worldview and a Roman Catholic social backdrop. After a scientific education in organismal biology and behavioral ecology, a series of life upheavals forced him to revisit everything.

Counseling led him back to religion and philosophy. In the 90s he studied transpersonal psychology with Stan Grof and certified as a Holotropic Breathwork facilitator. He later worked with social facilitators in consensus building around conservation and environmental issues, adding social psychology and mediation to his toolkit.

The insights in The Laws of ah! are the result of decades pursuing his own path, fueled by a deep interest in the fundamental issues of life — offered as a contribution to our never-ending quest, and an opportunity to test their validity.

About Zanimistic

"An emerging global cosmology that combines the best of our earliest spiritual practices with the latest zany understanding of reality, matter, and consciousness."

Zanimistic is a contraction of Zany Animistic — alluding to a cosmology that fuses ancient animistic and shamanic wisdom with modern experiential science (quantum physics, transpersonal psychology, consciousness research). Our spiritual awareness is coming full circle in tune with our understanding of cosmic laws, giving us a novel animated science — a Zanimistic Cosmology.

The four-leaf clover in the Zanimistic Editions logo represents the four basic domains of reality; the enveloping arc underscores the notion that our understanding of cosmos and spirit is coming full circle.
